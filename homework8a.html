Homework #8a: Interactive Story Assignment, Part 1

Story Description

My interactive story takes place in New York City at night. The main character is a student rushing home after class when they notice something unusual in the subway station. The story follows the character as they decide whether to investigate strange sounds, follow mysterious people, or leave the station quickly.

The user will make choices that change the direction of the story. Some choices lead to safety, while others lead to strange discoveries or unexpected danger. The story includes multiple paths and at least two different endings, so the user’s decisions shape the experience.
